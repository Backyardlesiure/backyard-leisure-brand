## Brand Identity

### Who We Are
Backyard Leisure is a **family-owned, community-rooted** retailer in Lethbridge, Alberta serving Southern Alberta for over 25 years. We sell hot tubs, swim spas, pools, saunas, and outdoor patio furniture — but more than products, we sell **the lifestyle of connection, relaxation, and outdoor living**.

### Brand Personality
- **Trustworthy** — 25 years in the community, family-owned, local experts
- **Aspirational** — We help people create their dream backyard sanctuary
- **Warm & Personal** — Not a big box store; we know our customers
- **Expert** — We educate, not just sell (Learning Center, Water Analysis, Service)
- **Community-focused** — Deeply rooted in Lethbridge and Southern Alberta

### Brand Promise
*We help families build backyard spaces for connection and relaxation.*

## Core Visual Principles

### Color Philosophy
The palette reflects **water, nature, and premium outdoor living** — drawn directly from the leaf/water drop logo:
- Brand Teal `#2F7769` anchors the brand with depth and trust (the dominant logo color)
- Aqua `#79CBC5` evokes water and the core product category (the water drop)
- Lime Green `#8FC855` adds natural energy and freshness (the leaf accent)
- Mint `#D0EFE9` provides soft, inviting backgrounds and highlights
- Off-white backgrounds keep things clean and inviting

Always load `references/colors.md` for exact hex values, usage rules, and do/don't examples.

### Typography Philosophy
The logo uses proprietary fonts; web and digital materials use close Google Fonts substitutes:
- **Logo only:** Alloy Bold ("BACKYARD") + Moonlight ("LEISURE") — these are for the logo, not for general use
- **Montserrat** for all web/digital headings — geometric, strong, closest web match to Alloy Bold's weight
- **Open Sans** for body copy — highly legible, friendly, accessible
- Never use decorative or script fonts in brand materials (except the logo itself)

Always load `references/typography.md` for size scales, weights, and spacing.

### Imagery Style
- **Real families** in real backyard settings — not stock photo perfection
- **Lethbridge/Alberta landscapes** where possible — mountains, prairie skies
- **Products in use** — not just product shots, but lifestyle in context
- **Seasonal range** — show hot tubs in winter snow (key differentiator for AB market)
- **Warm lighting** — golden hour, evening ambiance, steam rising from water
- **Avoid:** overly corporate, sterile, or generic imagery

### Layout Principles
- Generous white space — premium feel, not cluttered
- Photography-forward — let lifestyle images lead
- Clear visual hierarchy — headline → subhead → body → CTA
- CTAs are Brand Teal `#2F7769` on white, or white on Brand Teal

## Brand Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Lead with lifestyle benefits, not just product specs
- Reference the Southern Alberta community and climate
- Use warm, conversational language — we're neighbors, not a corporation
- Show families and social moments around the products
- Emphasize the 25-year trust and local expertise
- Use "backyard sanctuary" and "backyard space" as key phrases

### ❌ Don't
- Use cold, clinical, or overly technical language in public-facing copy
- Use competitor brand names
- Use colors outside the approved palette without approval
- Stretch, rotate, or alter the logo
- Use more than 2 font families in a single piece
- Make price the headline — lead with lifestyle, support with value
