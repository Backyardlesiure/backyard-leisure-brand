# Backyard Leisure — Brand Voice & Tone

---

## Voice vs. Tone

**Voice** is consistent — it's who we are.
**Tone** adapts — it shifts based on context, audience, and channel.

---

## Brand Voice

Backyard Leisure's voice is:

### 1. Warm and Personal
We've been your neighbor for 25+ years. We talk like it.
- ✅ "We'd love to help you find the right fit for your family."
- ❌ "Our team of professionals will assist you in making an optimal selection."

### 2. Knowledgeable Without Being Condescending
We're the experts — but we don't make you feel dumb for asking.
- ✅ "Hot tubs come in a range of sizes — a good rule of thumb is one seat per regular user."
- ❌ "The BTU output of the heater element determines thermal equilibration speed."

### 3. Aspirational, Not Salesy
We paint a picture of the lifestyle. We don't push.
- ✅ "Imagine stepping into warm water after a long Alberta winter day."
- ❌ "Buy now and save! Limited time offer ends soon!"

### 4. Community-Rooted
We're proud to be from Lethbridge and Southern Alberta.
- ✅ "We understand Alberta winters — that's why we carry hot tubs built for -40°C."
- ❌ Generic pan-Canadian or American-style copy that ignores our local context.

### 5. Honest and Direct
No puffery, no over-promising. We say what we mean.
- ✅ "This model is a great value pick — it doesn't have all the features of our premium line, but it'll last 15–20 years."
- ❌ "The absolute best hot tub on the market, guaranteed to change your life!"

## Tone by Channel

| Channel | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Website (homepage) | Inspirational, welcoming | "Build your sanctuary." |
| Product pages | Informative, helpful | Specs + lifestyle context |
| Blog/Learning Center | Educational, friendly | "Here's what you need to know..." |
| Social media (Instagram) | Warm, visual, casual | "Weekend goals 🌊 | #BackyardLeisure" |
| Social media (Facebook) | Community-oriented, conversational | Local events, customer stories |
| Email campaigns | Personal, value-driven | "As a valued customer..." |
| Service/support | Calm, reassuring, professional | "We've got you covered." |
| Promotions/sales | Enthusiastic, but never desperate | "Spring is coming — now's a great time to..." |
| Testimonials/reviews | Authentic — let customers speak naturally | Don't over-edit customer words |

## Key Messages by Product Category

### Hot Tubs
- Theme: **Year-round relaxation, family connection**
- Key phrases: "unwind", "soak away the day", "Alberta winters were made for this", "your daily ritual"
- Avoid: "jets" as the lead benefit — lead with experience, not features

### Swim Spas
- Theme: **Fitness meets relaxation, fits any backyard**
- Key phrases: "swim at home", "fitness and relaxation in one", "compact pool alternative"
- Audience angle: Active families, fitness enthusiasts, those with smaller yards

### Pools
- Theme: **Summer memories, family gatherings**
- Key phrases: "backyard destination", "summer starts here", "where memories are made"

### Patio Furniture
- Theme: **Complete the space, outdoor living year-round**
- Key phrases: "designed for the outdoors", "built to last Alberta seasons", "entertain in style"

### Saunas
- Theme: **Wellness, privacy, premium home retreat**
- Key phrases: "your personal wellness retreat", "the Nordic tradition", "health benefits of heat therapy"

## Writing Checklist

Before finalizing any copy, check:

- [ ] Does it sound like a neighbor, not a corporation?
- [ ] Does it lead with lifestyle/benefit, not just specs?
- [ ] Is it specific to Lethbridge/Alberta where relevant?
- [ ] Does it respect the customer's intelligence?
- [ ] Is it free of jargon (unless on a technical spec page)?
- [ ] Does it invite action without being pushy?
- [ ] Is it honest — no over-claiming?
- [ ] Does the CTA feel natural and helpful?

## Phrases We Use

| ✅ Use | ❌ Avoid |
|---|---|
| Backyard sanctuary | Dream backyard (overused) |
| Year-round relaxation | Luxury (too vague/pretentious) |
| Family connection | Investment (feels salesy) |
| Southern Alberta | Western Canada (too vague for us) |
| Get started | Click here |
| We'd love to help | Don't hesitate to contact us |
| Built for Alberta winters | Suitable for cold climates |
| Over 25 years | Since [year] (less powerful) |
| Your backyard, your rules | (No equivalent — too casual) |

## Emoji Usage (Social Only)
- Use sparingly — 1-2 per post maximum
- Approved: 🌊 💧 ☀️ 🏊 🛁 🌿 ❄️ 🔥 ⭐ ✅
- Avoid: Anything that feels juvenile, trendy, or off-brand
- Never use emoji in formal documents, emails, or website copy
