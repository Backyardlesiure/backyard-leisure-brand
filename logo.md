## Clear Space

- Maintain clear space of **at least the height of the "B" in "Backyard"** on all sides
- Never crowd the logo with other elements, text, or images
- On mobile, allow slightly less clear space but never zero

---

## Minimum Sizes

| Format | Minimum Width |
|---|---|
| Digital/Web | 120px |
| Print | 1.25 inches / 32mm |
| Favicon/icon | 32px (icon only version) |

---

## Approved Logo Placements

- Top-left corner of all web pages (navigation bar)
- Top-center of email headers
- Bottom-right or bottom-left of print materials (with tagline if space allows)
- Center of presentation title slides

---

## ❌ Logo Don'ts

- Do NOT stretch or distort the logo proportions
- Do NOT rotate the logo
- Do NOT add drop shadows, glows, or effects
- Do NOT place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds (use a color bar underneath)
- Do NOT recreate the logo in a different font
- Do NOT use the logo smaller than minimum size — use the icon version instead
- Do NOT use the logo in any color other than the approved versions
- Do NOT place a colored box or shape behind the logo unless it's an approved brand color

---

## Co-branding with Partner Brands

Backyard Leisure carries several partner brands. When displaying partner logos alongside the BL logo:

- BL logo should always be **equal or larger** than partner logos
- Maintain a visible separation between logos (at least 2x the BL logo height)
- Partner logos in approved locations: product pages, brochures, showroom signage

**Partner brands carried:**
- Hot Spring® Spas
- Freeflow Spas®
- Beachcomber Hot Tubs
- Hydropool
- Endless Pools®
- Kona Pools
- Tylö Saunas
- Ebel, OW Lee, CRP, Homecrest, Mallin, Ratana, Treasure Garden (patio furniture)
- Covana

Always use partner brand logos exactly as provided by those manufacturers.
